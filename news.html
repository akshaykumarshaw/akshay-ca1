Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM):

Aim: The Jal Jeevan Mission aims to provide piped water supply to all rural households by 2024.
Objectives: It focuses on ensuring potable water supply to every household, prioritizing water quality, sustainability, and community participation.
Strategies: The mission emphasizes decentralized water management, groundwater recharge, rainwater harvesting, and infrastructure development for water supply and treatment.


Atal Bhujal Yojana (ABHY):

Aim: The Atal Bhujal Yojana aims to improve groundwater management and promote sustainable groundwater use in identified priority areas.
Objectives: It focuses on community participation, demand-side management, and efficient use of groundwater resources through regulatory measures, capacity building, and technology adoption.
Strategies: The scheme includes activities such as aquifer mapping, water budgeting, participatory groundwater management, incentivizing water conservation practices, and promoting recharge structures.


Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY):

Aim: The PMKSY aims to enhance water use efficiency in agriculture, improve farm productivity, and ensure integrated water resource management.
Objectives: It focuses on promoting micro-irrigation, water-saving technologies, sustainable groundwater management, and water conservation practices in agriculture.
Components: The scheme comprises four components: Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme (AIBP), Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP), Per Drop More Crop (PDMC), and Watershed Development (WD).



National Water Mission (NWM):

Aim: The National Water Mission aims to conserve water resources, minimize wastage, and ensure equitable distribution of water across sectors.
Objectives: It focuses on improving water use efficiency, promoting water conservation practices, enhancing water storage capacity, and implementing sustainable water management practices.
Strategies: The mission promotes rainwater harvesting, watershed management, water-efficient agriculture, water recycling, and reuse, as well as groundwater recharge initiatives. Website: National Water Mission
